Sparkling Breakfast

Wake up to the sound of live piano music and dainty effervescence of sparkling wine. Feel the gentle ocean breeze while we treat you to a delightful presentation of fresh island fare to satisfy your morning cravings.

Cuisine

International Buffet with free flow of sparkling wine and local specialties

Location

Salinda Restaurant

Opening Hours

06:30 to 10:30

For reservation or any inquiries, please contact us via emailrestaurant@salindaresort.com

Menu

BREAKFAST ON PHU QUOC ISLAND

GOOD MORNING BUBBLY

Salinda’s Sparkling Breakfast with free-flow of sparkling wine from selective boutique wineries will enhance your holiday weekend, making it a truly luxurious experience. In addition, guests are invited to make their own mimosa cocktail by combining freshly squeezed orange juice with a chilled sparkling wine to toast a new day of their vacation. Tiny treats as this are essential if you are looking for the bespoke pampering component of your stay.

FEEL ADVENTUROUS BUT NEVER HOMESICK

Salinda chefs will treat you to a delightful presentation of island fare to satisfy any of your morning cravings. With a mouthwatering seasonal menu, the buffet’s appetizer station features a sushi corner, freshly made salads or organic vegetables for guests to mix with various homemade dressings, and a wide selection of cheeses and cold cuts from Western Europe.

Every day you can go on a new journey through Vietnam by trying different authentic noodle soups of the day like phở, bánh canh, bún bò huế, hủ tiếu, bún riêu. On the other hand, jump-start your day with a protein-rich egg favorite, freshly cracked to each order. Salinda’s Eggs Benedict (poached egg with fresh hollandaise sauce on home-baked bread) is a must-do. Dig into a hearty selection of hot comfort dishes that change on daily basis: dim sum, congee (cháo), curated sausages, stir-fries, and stews.

If you cannot resist a toasted croissant in the morning, you are in for quite a treat – all breads, muesli, Danish pastries are homemade and freshly baked, pancakes, and waffles prepared a-la-minute, while all tropical jams are home stewed from fresh fruits. Besides, for health-conscious guests, nothing beats a bowl of Greek yogurt mixed with homemade granola and a dash of natural honey dripping from an Australian honeycomb.

THE SOUND OF THE MORNING

Is there anything better than waking up to a freshly made breakfast accompanied by a sense-awakening melody from the pianist on a white grand piano? With the soothing sound of waves drenching the beach in the background, an experience that will surely wash any worries away. When the morning sun softly caresses the gardens, guests can feel the inviting aroma of freshly brewed Vietnamese or Italian coffee through the mid-morning breeze on the terrace, sweeping through the majestic tropical trees. Indeed, the complete panorama with light blue interiors is a boutique designed to enhance and enrich the views of the oceanfront, creating a sense of freedom and wanderlust hundreds of miles away from the cities.
